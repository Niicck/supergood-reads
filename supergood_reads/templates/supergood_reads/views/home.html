What is SupergoodReads?

On the technical side, the purpose of this site is for me to experiment with some new ways of making Django applications. These are features that I haven't seen in too many other public projects, so I hope they're interesting/useful to you.

One of my goals was to see how far I could enhance a Django templates before needing to swap out the frontend for a totally decoupled React/Vue.js app.

  • ✅ Autocomplete Django form fields using Vue.js Components
